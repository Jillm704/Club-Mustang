Membership

Coupe Package

The Coupe Package is our starter-pack paid subscription service. Many users start with this package and later upgrade to our premium packages. With this package, you will :

Create your profile to join the community

Receive an official membership pin and bumper sticker

Have access to our weekly newsletter and free download of all images, including our professional gallery

$19.99/Year

Mach-1 Package

The Mach-1 Package is the next step up from the Coupe Package, and the package of choice for many of our subscribers. The Mach-1 package will give you additional premium benefits, including :

All Coupe Package benefits automatically included

Receive a yearly raffle prize from our shop items and free shipping on select items

Ability to participate in Club Mustang's annual Mustang Raffle, where we choose one lucky subscriber to receive a fully-restored mustang selected by members' popular vote. You may enter numerous times for a separate fee.

$49.99/Year

Shelby Package

The Shelby Package is our most premium subscription plan available to Club Mustang users. While it may be the priciest, it is also the most inclusive package and contains every possible benefit we have to offer :

All Coupe and Mach-1 Package benefits automatically included

Receive a quarterly Shelby-exclusive raffle prize in addition to the yearly raffle prize, as well as free shipping on ALL of our store items

Enter our annual Mustang Raffle twenty-five times at absolutely no extra cost, and at a discounted fee rate for each additional entry past twenty-five

Every quarter you enter to win a free five-day trip to Ford World Headquarters in Dearborn, Michigan, where you will get a full tour and a guaranteed test-drive of the entire Ford Performance lineup. Inlcudes both modern and classic Ford creations.

$149.99/Year
